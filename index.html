Godavari

Seetha is an independent, strong, and career-oriented woman who runs a not so successful fashion store. She reluctantly agrees to the marriage proposal because of her parents. However, the marriage doesn’t work out as the groom’s family rejects her. Frustrated, Geetha decides to go on a solo cruise trip to the Godavari where she meets Ram, who is already in love with his cousin. How the beautiful Godavari breeze brings them together forms the rest of the story.

  • Kamalini Mukherjee, Sumanth.
  • Available On: Hotstar
  • IMDb Rating: 7.9/10

Sreeram (Sumanth) is a principled chap with an inclination towards politics. With an altruistic attitude, he goes out of his way to help others. Seeta (Kamalinee Mukherjee) is a girl who does not want to confine to home and wants to live independently as an entrepreneur. Sreeram is seriously fascinated by his maradalu Raji (Neetu Chandra) and wants to marry her. But she feels that he is not her kind of guy. Raji's parents settle her marriage with an IPS officer (Kamal Kamaraju). Seeta meets Sreeram and his relatives on their boat journey from Rajahmundry to Bhadrachalam. The rest of the story is all about how Sreeram and Seeta realize how perfect they are for each other.

Mahanati

Based on a biopic of Superstar Savitri who acted in multiple languages, winning the hearts of the masses. This movie explores the rise and fall of Savitramma through the lens of Anthony, a cameraman, and Madhuravani, a journalist. The larger-than-life character was beautifully portrayed by Keerthi Suresh, who achieved a national award with this film. Savitri’s is a story of love, compassion, confidence, success, and endurance. This film also parallelly narrates the story of journalist Madhuravani, who is inspired by the journey of Savitri.

  • Keerthi Suresh, Dulquer Salman, Samantha, Vijay Devarakonda
  • Available On: Hotstar
  • IMDb Rating: 8/10

Savitri, the most admired actress from South Indian cinema, goes through various tragic incidents in her personal life. Unable to cope with depression, she eventually becomes an alcoholic.
